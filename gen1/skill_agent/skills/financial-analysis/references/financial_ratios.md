# Financial Ratios Reference

## Profitability Ratios
| Ratio          | Formula                                    | Notes                          |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Gross Margin   | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100          | Higher = better pricing power  |
| EBITDA Margin  | EBITDA / Revenue × 100                    | Operating efficiency           |
| Net Margin     | Net Income / Revenue × 100               | Bottom line efficiency         |
| ROA            | Net Income / Total Assets                 | Asset utilisation              |
| ROE            | Net Income / Shareholder Equity           | Return on invested equity      |
| ROIC           | NOPAT / Invested Capital                  | True capital efficiency        |

## Liquidity Ratios
| Ratio          | Formula                                    | Healthy Range                  |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Current Ratio  | Current Assets / Current Liabilities      | 1.5–3.0                        |
| Quick Ratio    | (Cash + AR) / Current Liabilities         | > 1.0                          |
| Cash Ratio     | Cash / Current Liabilities                | > 0.5                          |

## Leverage Ratios
| Ratio          | Formula                                    | Notes                          |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| D/E Ratio      | Total Debt / Total Equity                 | < 2.0 generally safe           |
| Interest Cover | EBIT / Interest Expense                   | > 3x comfortable               |
| Net Debt/EBITDA| (Debt - Cash) / EBITDA                   | < 3x preferred                 |

## Valuation Ratios
| Ratio          | Formula                                    | Notes                          |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| P/E            | Share Price / EPS                         | Compare to sector average      |
| P/S            | Market Cap / Revenue                      | Useful for pre-profit companies|
| P/B            | Share Price / Book Value per Share        | < 1 may indicate undervalue    |
| EV/EBITDA      | Enterprise Value / EBITDA                 | 10–15x typical                 |
| EV/Revenue     | Enterprise Value / Revenue               | Useful for SaaS (5–15x)        |
| PEG Ratio      | P/E / EPS Growth Rate                    | < 1 = undervalued growth       |
